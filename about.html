ABOUT ME

I am a digital artist from Sydney, Australia who works with code based software to create my artwork. I come from a traditional art and design background, but now find myself spending hours obsessing over the numbers that drive these artworks. I am especially interested in creating works that feel organic in structure, replicating algorithms derived from nature and natural occurences. I sometimes combine my photographic works into video installations, with the idea of deconstructing the images from data captures.
